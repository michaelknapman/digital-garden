# Jun Ishikawa and Hirokazu Ando

Selected works by Jun Ishikawa and Hirokazu Ando.

Jun Ishikawa is more prolific in this list because he was the sole composer of more games.  
Also he seems to have been given the responsibility of more level themes than Hirokazu Ando.
Therefore Jun has more chance to shine in the spotlight.
